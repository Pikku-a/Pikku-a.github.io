Game description

A chill turn-based strategy rougelite game with good lofi hiphop music.

Features
- Turn-based strategy battles
- Infinite replayability + 50 hand-designed levels
- Learn unique sword skills, like Teleport Attack
- Lofi hiphop music
- Relaxing atmosphere
- Customization: Buy new skins, outfits and swords
- Find out the story about your father's disappearance and the monsters' origin
- Collect treasure that you find from the basement to your home
- Ideal to play while listening to something

Genre

Indie, Turn-based Strategy, Tactics, Rougelite, Dungeon Crawler, RPG, Puzzle, Adventure

Windows / Linux - Steam & itch.io
